# Viking Vengeance — What You Need To Do

- **Infantry and Cavalry only** — no Archers, ever. They do not damage Vikings and waste kill credit
- Clear your infirmary before the event starts — injured troops cannot fight
- Free up all marches — nothing gathering, garrisoning elsewhere, or mid-march
- Keep your best defensive heroes (Amadeus, Helga, Howard, Jabel, Saul, Quinn) on your barricade at home
- Use your remaining heroes to lead your reinforcement marches

# Reinforcing Alliance Members

- Agree on who you are reinforcing before the event starts — coordinate in chat
- Send your Infantry and Cavalry marches to reinforce your partner early and leave them there
- Infantry first — they front-line and secure the most kills
- You and the person you reinforce both earn points from the same kills, so reinforce as many people as you can
- Try to focus on reinforcing people who are online (you will get more points this way)

# HQ Waves — Waves 10 and 20

Top 15 members online defend the plains HQ.

- After wave 9 is fully done — recall one strong march and send it to defend HQ
- Once wave 10 clears — recall and send it back to reinforce your partners
- Repeat after wave 19 for wave 20
- Infantry and Cavalry only, no Archers
- Do not recall early, wait until the previous wave is completely finished

# Do Not Heal During the Event

Do not heal troops between waves. Wait until all 20 waves are done before clearing your infirmary. Healing mid-event pulls troops back to your city instead of keeping them reinforcing others.

# If Your City Gets Burned Twice

Vikings will no longer target you directly. Keep your marches out reinforcing alliance members — you still earn points through their kills. Do not log off.

# Before the Event Checklist

- [ ] Infirmary cleared
- [ ] Best defensive heroes on your barricade
- [ ] Reinforcement partners sorted in chat
- [ ] All marches free
- [ ] Infantry and Cavalry only 60/40/00 — no Archers
