# Swordland Showdown — Alliance Member Guide

Swordland is one of the most important events we do as an alliance. No troops are permanently lost here so there is zero reason not to go all in. Read this, know your role, and show up ready. Leadership will handle registrations and timings — your job is to be prepared and contribute.

# How You Earn Points

Points come from two places — buildings and combat. Buildings are always the priority as they earn both you and the alliance points. Combat only earns you personal points so do not ignore fights but never at the cost of building control.

- **First Capture** — massive one-time bonus for you and the alliance, be first wherever you can
- **Holding Buildings** — steady ongoing points for as long as you hold them, Swordshrine and Sanctums earn the most
- **Loot Crates** — spawn when we flip enemy buildings, grab them the second they appear
- **Undercellars** — burst points that show up randomly in Phase 3, use the magnifying glass icon on the minimap to find them
- **Attacking** — 80 personal points per 10k power defeated
- **Defending** — 40 personal points per 10k power defeated

# Know Your Role

Leadership will assign you a role before the battle. Stick to it.

**Tank / Defender (2 groups)**
- Garrison the important buildings — Swordshrine, Sanctums, Royal Stables, Bell Tower
- Work with the other Tanks to make sure nothing is left undefended
- Position your city close so you can reinforce quickly

**DPS / Attacker**
- Go after their strongest players first — take out the biggest threats early
- Use teleports to hit enemy cities when they least expect it
- In the late game start rallying buildings to force loot crates

**Support**
- Keep moving, do not sit still
- Grab every loot crate and Arsenal Supply you can reach
- Hit the Undercellars hard in Phase 3
- Fill in wherever defence needs help
- Use the Troop Encampment Dance when you are outmatched (see below)

# How the Battle Plays Out

**Phase 1 — First 15 Minutes**
This phase sets the tone. Move fast and do not hesitate.
- Your first teleport is free — use it immediately to get to your assigned building
- Priority: Royal Stables first, then Bell Tower, then a Sanctum, then Abbeys
- Spread out — do not pile onto one building and leave others empty
- Even a weak defender on a building stops a solo capture — be there

**Phase 2 — Minutes 15 to 45**
The big buildings open up. Stay disciplined.
- Hall of Reformation opens — grab this before we touch the Swordshrine, the 15% damage boost matters
- Do not rush the Swordshrine the second it unlocks, we go when we are coordinated and ready
- Use the Mercenary Camp to wear down enemy garrisons before we assault

**Phase 3 — Final 15 Minutes**
This is where matches are won and lost. Stay focused.
- Undercellars are now on the map — small fast squads should be hunting these down
- We make our coordinated Swordshrine push here, attack team goes first, support follows seconds behind
- Last 5 minutes: rally enemy buildings to drain their points, collect every Arsenal Supply on the ground

# Tactics Worth Knowing

**Troop Encampment Dance**
If you are about to get steamrolled and cannot win the fight, do not just sit there and take it.
- Encamp all your troops at a safe nearby location before they arrive
- Let them burn your empty city
- Teleport somewhere new and get back in the fight
- You keep your troops and stay useful

**The 12-Minute Healing Reset**
All Swordland injuries heal for free. Use this.
- Fight until your troops are worn down
- Leave via the VS banner — "Leave Battlefield"
- Heal everything for free
- Wait out the 12-minute cooldown and rejoin at full strength
- Do not waste a whole hour on depleted troops when you can reset

**When We Are Outmatched**
Do not just feed the enemy kills. Play smart.
- Find isolated weaker enemies and pick your fights
- Earn your personal points then leave properly via the VS banner
- Wait the 12 minutes, rejoin and repeat
- Good personal performance still earns solid rewards even in the Loser's Bracket
