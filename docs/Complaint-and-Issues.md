# Complaint and Issues

If there is ever an issue please make sure to flag it with myself or one of the R4 team. We are happy to be the impartial party that can help find resolutions to any issues but we only know what we know if you tell us.

# What to do if there is an issue

- Speak to an R4 or AChubbyWiggle as soon as possible. 
- Explain clearly what happened.
- If possible include screenshots so we can see the full situation.
- Do not start arguments in alliance chat.
- Do not retaliate or escalate the issue while it is being looked into.
- Give leadership time to review both sides properly.

# What should be reported

- Problems with alliance members.
- Repeated disrespect or drama in chat.
- Breaking alliance rules or event instructions. 
- Attacks on alliance members, academies, allies or NAP without approval. 
- Ongoing behaviour that is damaging the alliance or causing issues for others.
- Any concerns that you think leadership should be aware of. 

# How we will handle it

- We will listen to what happened. 
- We may ask questions or ask for screenshots if needed. 
- We will look at the situation as fairly as possible. 
- If needed we will speak to both sides before making a decision. 
- Leadership decisions are expected to be respected once made. 

# What not to do

- Do not argue in alliance chat. 
- Do not spam leadership with half the story and leave details out. 
- Do not take matters into your own hands. 
- Do not turn personal issues into alliance drama. 
- Do not ignore a problem and then only report it once it becomes much bigger. 

# Final note

We all want a strong, respectful and successful alliance. Problems will happen from time to time, but the best way to solve them is early, calmly and properly.
