# KVK Battle — Alliance Member Guide

KVK battle is won by coordination, not by everyone doing their own thing. Stay calm, follow calls, move together, and focus on what helps the alliance win. We try to have a few people in discord - Join us and have a laugh

# Your job during KVK battle

- Follow R4/R5 and kingdom calls straight away
- Watch alliance mail, chat, markers, and flags
- Move with the alliance, not ahead of it and not away from it
- Focus on the current objective, not random fights
- If leadership changes the plan, adapt quickly

# How to move as an alliance

- Stay close enough to reinforce, rally, and support quickly
- Do not scatter all over the map chasing solo fights
- If the alliance is setting up for a push, be in position before the call happens
- If leadership says regroup, regroup fully and fast
- If we are resetting position, do not be the one left behind feeding troops alone

# Castle and turret priority

- Castle and turrets matter more than random kills
- If we are assigned a turret, take it seriously and get there fast
- If we hold castle but lose turret control, we will suffer for it
- If we cannot take castle yet, control the turrets and support the next push
- Do not leave objectives empty because you want to farm points elsewhere
- KVK is won by controlling what matters, not by chasing whatever is nearest

# Rally discipline

- Join rallies quickly when called
- Fill rallies properly and do not leave them short because you joined the wrong one
- Pay attention to which rally needs filling first
- Let the strongest players lead main rallies and structure fights
- If a rally is for an important structure, that takes priority over side fights
- Do not sit with idle marches when rallies are being called

# Reinforcing properly

- Reinforce the players and structures you are told to reinforce
- If you are not a main rally lead, support the players who are
- Keep your march useful where it matters most instead of wasting it on random map fights
- If you are asked to swap targets, do it quickly
- Good reinforcements often decide whether we hold or lose an objective

# Picking the right fights

- Fight where it helps the alliance push, defend, or hold position
- Do not chase enemy marches across the map and drag yourself out of position
- Do not take fights that pull you away from castle, turrets, or alliance markers
- If the call is to hold, stop trying to make hero plays
- If the call is to push, be ready to commit with the group
- Random kills mean very little if the alliance loses the objective

# If you are lower power

- Reinforce stronger players and key structures
- Fill rallies quickly and properly
- Stay close to alliance movement so your support is useful
- Your job is to make the alliance stronger, not to prove something alone
- Smart support play is far more useful than bad solo fighting

# During active calls

- Keep chat clear and useful
- Report what matters, enemy movements, structure pressure, and if a rally needs filling
- Do not spam panic messages in the middle of a push
- Do not argue with calls while the battle is happening
- If you missed a call, check markers and mail fast and catch up
- Clear communication wins fights

# Troop management during battle

- Watch your infirmary and heal before it becomes a problem
- Do not keep feeding troops into bad fights once leadership says stop
- If we need to reset, preserve troops for the next push
- Keep enough teleports and healing to stay useful for the full battle
- Do not burn everything in the first part of the fight and become dead weight later

# Common mistakes that lose battles

- Teleporting without orders
- Chasing kills instead of holding objectives
- Ignoring turret calls
- Joining the wrong rally
- Being too far away to reinforce in time
- Feeding troops after the reset call
- Going silent and not reacting to updated instructions

# Quick checklist

- [ ] Watching chat, mail, markers, and flags
- [ ] Moving with the alliance
- [ ] Prioritising castle and turrets over random fights
- [ ] Filling rallies quickly and correctly
- [ ] Reinforcing the right targets
- [ ] Staying in position for the next call
- [ ] Managing healing and not feeding troops
